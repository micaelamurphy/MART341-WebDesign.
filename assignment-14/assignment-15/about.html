Why Nike?

I chose to cover my favorite nike shoes because one of my favorite hobbies is collecting and researching different nike products. I like to keep the shoes in top quality as well as sell them when I don't wear them as much. For me, shoes are the way I express myself in my outfits. Usually I wear pretty color nuetral outfits, so I make sure my shoes always have a pop of color. I own over fifteen pairs of Nike shoes right now and plan to keep buying and selling the shoes.

Advice to Beginners

If you are interested in starting to collect and sell shoes the best advice I've learned is to be patient. Selling shoes take some time, but there is someone ALWAYS interested. I also would advise to keep finances in mind. Sometimes, buying can be quite the expense, so only buy if you think you can afford it and then turn around and make a profit from the shoe. FInally, I would advise to always do research on the shoes you are buying, so you can be an informed buyer and upkeeper as well as a seller when the time comes to sell the shoe. Just enjoy the process!
